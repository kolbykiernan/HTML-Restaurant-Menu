Origin Story

H-Thai-ML was born from a passion for sharing the authentic tastes and aromas of Thailand with the diverse and discerning palates of San Diego. Our journey began with a dream to recreate the warmth and hospitality of Thai homes, where meals are not just sustenance, but expressions of love and tradition. Drawing inspiration from the bustling street markets of Bangkok, the tranquil beaches of Phuket, and the lush landscapes of Chiang Mai, we embarked on a mission to bring the essence of Thailand to your plate.
